Final Project Process


For my final project, I made a camera dolly that is attachable to my tripod. The code is written with Arduino on a metro board. I bought a metal zinc bar that would be strong enough to support my camera. Then I modeled a 3D piece that could fit in my tripod slot and could be attached to the metal bar. The code ran 2 stepper motors in the same direction so I placed 1 motor on each end of the bar with a string wrapped around both. I struggled to make the camera stable; I tried using various amounts of cardboard, but ended up just adding a bit of tape to the bottom of the camera to keep it from scrapping.
