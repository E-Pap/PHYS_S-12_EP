Programable Electronics


Starting with the second part of the assignment, I wanted to use a photogrammatry app to scan my Darth Vader cookie jar. I ended up trying four different apps: Scandy Pro 3D, Qlone 3D, Capture, & Bellus 3D. Of these, Capture was the one I had the most success with. The only downside to this particular app is that it can only save the scans as a 3D piont cloud. I will continue to try different ways of converting these files.

For the other part of the assignment, I began woring on a 3D model of a component for my final project. I am planning my final project to be an attachable track for my tripod that would be able to roll my camera across at a programmable speed. The main part of my track is a 1 yard metal bar. I modeld both the bar, and a connector piece that can be printed. The gcode and stl for the connector is in the link below.
